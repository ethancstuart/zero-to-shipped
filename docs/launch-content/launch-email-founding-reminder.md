## Editing notes for Ethan

- **Fill the `{{X}}`, `{{Y}}`, `{{Z}}` placeholders with real numbers before sending.** Pull them from `/admin/funnel` or your Stripe dashboard. If you don't have impressive numbers yet, be honest about it — "I'm at 3 students and 0 shipped builds" is more trustworthy than fake social proof.
- **Subject line**: the call is between scarcity framing (#1) or transparency framing (#2). I'd default to #2 unless you actually have fewer than 30 spots left — real scarcity reads as factual, manufactured scarcity reads as sleazy.
- **No "last chance" language**. Never. You're building a brand that's not that kind of brand.
- **Don't send if**: every founding spot sold out in 48 hours. In that case, send a different email — "We sold out the founding tier in 48 hours, thank you, here's what's next" — which I'll draft separately if it happens.
- **Send time**: 10am ET Thursday April 30. Tuesday's launch email went at 8am; staggering this one two hours later avoids looking robotic.
- **Reply management**: the "hit reply if stuck" line in the PS is the single highest-value sentence. Every reply is a UX test. Read all of them, fix what you can in the first 72 hours post-launch.
