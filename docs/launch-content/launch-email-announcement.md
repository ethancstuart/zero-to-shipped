## Editing notes for Ethan

- **Voice**: personal, not corporate. "Hey, six months ago I told a handful of people..." — that's the register. Don't let it drift into marketing-speak.
- **Links**: exactly 3 — homepage, preview (primary), pricing. No tracking link soup.
- **PS line**: keep it. "I read every response personally" makes this feel real. If that's not true on launch day because of volume, change it to "I read every response in the first 48 hours."
- **Send time**: 8am ET. One hour before the LinkedIn post goes live so inbox openers see it first.
- **Segment strategy**: send to the FULL waitlist at once. Don't A/B the launch email — not enough volume for stat sig and you lose the "everyone got it at the same time" energy.
- **Founding coupon**: if your Stripe flow uses a coupon code, include it in the email. Otherwise the /pricing page already shows $99 to logged-in users until the first 100 are sold, so no code needed.
- **Follow-up**: the founding-coupon-reminder email (separate draft) goes to non-converters 48 hours later. Do not send it sooner.
