Key Decisions Baked In

  • Vercel KV for storage — no separate DB to manage. Stores post log + pillar config. Buffer is source of truth for scheduling.
  • 24h window — posts queue to Buffer 24h before publish time. Admin UI shows anything in that window, editable until it goes live.
  • Pillar rotation — generator reads last 7 days of posts from KV to avoid repeating pillars or topics.
  • Beehiiv separate — newsletter is weekly, generated on its own cron, drafted in Beehiiv (not auto-published — Beehiiv API creates a draft, you hit send).
  • Standalone repo — zts-marketing, own Vercel project, own env vars. Zero coupling to zerotoship.app.
